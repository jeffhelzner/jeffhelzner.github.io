SEU Sensitivity — Overview

This is a high-level, conversational overview of the SEU Sensitivity project. For technical reports and code, see the project pages linked below.

  • SEU Sensitivity project site and technical reports
  • Source repository on GitHub

Key ideas (short)

The project uses a Bayesian model to explore how deviations from SEU-optimal choices affect decision outcomes, and what kinds of sensitivity are most consequential for real-world decision-making.

Why this matters

Understanding sensitivity to SEU provides a bridge between theoretical notions of rationality and applied decision-making problems, including AI-assisted decisions.
